ABOUT ME

I am a self-taught web developer with a love for solving problems and drinking coffee. I grew up enjoying puzzles, word and number games, strategy games, and pretty much anything that made me think. Web development has been a great outlet for these passions. The coffee addiction came later in life, but is currently no less vital to who I am.

The track that I followed included HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Ruby, and Ruby on Rails. I was also encouraged to look into freeCodeCamp (another free plug freeCodeCamp). Here I learned more about responsive web design techniques and eventually React.
